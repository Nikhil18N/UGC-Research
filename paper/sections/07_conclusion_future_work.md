The study demonstrates a practical ML-guided alternative to fixed heuristic hybrid filtering for hot/cold tuple classification in in-memory workloads. Lightweight models can improve prediction quality while remaining computationally feasible for small-scale systems research.

Future work includes:
1. Online/incremental learning under concept drift.
2. Direct integration with Redis/SQLite in-memory execution traces.
3. Multi-class tiering beyond binary hot/cold labels.
4. End-to-end latency and memory benchmarking on larger datasets.
