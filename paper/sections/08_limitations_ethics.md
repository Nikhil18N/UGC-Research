## Limitations
1. Synthetic workloads may not capture all production access dynamics.
2. Hot/cold labels are proxy labels derived from access frequency.
3. Evaluation focuses on classification and simulated impact rather than full DB engine integration.

## Threats to Validity
- Internal validity: metric outcomes can vary with workload skew parameters.
- External validity: results may differ in real systems with concurrent and mixed query patterns.
- Construct validity: hotness definitions can vary across applications.

## Ethical and Responsible Use
This project does not use personal or sensitive data. Reproducibility artifacts are provided to support transparent evaluation.
